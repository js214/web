The SG380 Series RF Signal Generators use a unique, innovative architecture (Rational Approximation Frequency Synthesis) to deliver ultra-high frequency resolution (1 µHz), excellent phase noise, and versatile modulation capabilities. Standard models generate sine waves from DC to 2.025 GHz (SG382), 4.05 GHz (SG384), or 6.075 GHz (SG386).

### Frequency Performance

The generators achieve 1 µHz resolution at any frequency with frequency stability better than 1 × 10⁻¹¹ (1 s Allan variance) and switching speed under 8 ms to within 1 ppm.

### Output Options

The BNC output covers DC to 62.5 MHz. The N-type output spans 950 kHz to the respective GHz limit of each model. Power range extends from +16.5 dBm to −110 dBm.

### Modulation

Amplitude modulation (AM), frequency modulation (FM), phase modulation (ΦM), and pulse modulation are all supported. Linear and logarithmic frequency sweeps with configurable start/stop frequencies and rates are included.

### Timebase

A standard OCXO timebase provides excellent stability. An optional rubidium oscillator (Opt. 04) is available for applications requiring the highest stability.

### Computer Interfaces

GPIB, RS-232, and Ethernet interfaces with up to nine saved configurations provide full remote control.
