The FS725 Rubidium Frequency Standard provides a stable, accurate 10 MHz reference.

### Rubidium Oscillator

The instrument achieves frequency accuracy of ±5 × 10⁻¹¹ and aging less than 5 × 10⁻¹¹ per month.

### Outputs

Three rear-panel outputs provide 10 MHz sine wave (13 dBm into 50 Ω), 10 MHz CMOS square wave, and 1 PPS.

### Monitoring

RS-232 interface allows remote monitoring of oscillator parameters. A Windows monitoring application is available. Compact half-rack enclosure with optional rack mounting kits.
