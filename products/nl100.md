The NL100 Nitrogen Laser provides pulsed ultraviolet output at 337 nm for spectroscopy, dye laser pumping, and laboratory use.

The laser delivers pulses with peak power greater than 150 kW at repetition rates up to 20 Hz. Pulse duration is approximately 3.5 ns. The output beam is linearly polarized.

The instrument includes a built-in trigger generator and accepts external TTL triggers. No external gas supply is needed — the laser operates with ambient air.
