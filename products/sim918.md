The SIM918 Precision Voltage Source provides a stable, low-noise DC output for the SIM900 mainframe.

Four voltage ranges (±1 V, ±10 V, ±100 V, and ±100 V with 10× output) with 4½-digit resolution. Output noise is less than 3 µVrms (0.1 Hz to 10 Hz) on the 1 V range. The output can scan linearly between two voltages at adjustable rates.
