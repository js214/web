The SIM921 AC Resistance Bridge provides high-resolution resistance measurement for cryogenic thermometry in the SIM900 mainframe.

The bridge measures resistance from 2 Ω to 2 MΩ with resolution better than 1 ppm. AC excitation at 15.9 Hz minimizes thermoelectric errors while keeping self-heating low. Excitation current is adjustable from 10 nA to 3 mA. Designed for precision temperature measurement using RTDs, germanium, and ruthenium oxide sensors.
