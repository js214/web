The SR620 Universal Time Interval Counter delivers 25 ps single-shot time resolution across a 1.3 GHz frequency range with 11-digit frequency resolution (1 s gate time).

### Measurement Capabilities

The counter performs time interval, frequency, pulse-width, rise/fall time, period, phase, and event measurements. Time intervals achieve 25 ps rms resolution, making it among the highest-resolution counters available. Frequency measurements span from 0.001 Hz to 1.3 GHz.

### Graphical Display

Unlike conventional counters with numeric-only displays, the SR620 offers live graphical presentations in three formats: histograms showing value distributions, strip charts of successive measurement means, or strip-charts displaying jitter values. Up to 250 data points display simultaneously.

### Statistics and Analysis

The instrument calculates mean, standard deviation, Allan variance, minimum, and maximum values from sample sizes ranging from one to one million measurements per set.

### Timebases

Standard crystal and optional ovenized timebases are available. GPIB and RS-232 interfaces provide full remote control.
