The SR10, SR11 & SR12 Switchers bring multiple-channel I/O capability to the SR1 Audio Analyzer. These switching systems allow you to expand the two-input / two-output channels of SR1 by up to 192 channels. They can also be used in general purpose systems to control almost any number of signals.
