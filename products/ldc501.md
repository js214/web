The LDC500 series laser diode controllers provide combined laser diode current source and TEC temperature control in a single instrument.

### Laser Diode Driver

The controller offers 100 mA, 500 mA or 2 A current source options with ultra-low drift (<10 ppm/°C) and extremely low current noise (<0.3 µA for the LDC500). Laser diode protection includes slow start turn-on, adjustable current limits, and adjustable compliance voltage.

### TEC Controller

The temperature controller delivers 36 W of output power with ultra-high stability (0.0005 °C/°C). It supports thermistor, RTD, and IC sensors. Auto-tuning of PID loop parameters is included, and a unique "Bumpless Transfer" feature enables dynamic switching between constant current and constant power modes without shutdown.

### User Interface

An intuitive front panel features dedicated two-line blue alphanumeric displays alongside bright 5-digit green LED displays for current and temperature monitoring. Up to nine instrument configurations can be stored in non-volatile RAM.

### Computer Control

GPIB, RS-232, and Ethernet interfaces allow full remote operation. A TTL trigger output enables synchronization with external equipment.
