The SR400 is a dual-channel gated photon counter for time-resolved photon counting experiments.

The instrument provides two independent counting channels with gate windows from 5 ns to 1 s. Gates can be independently positioned, allowing simultaneous measurement of signal and background. Count rates up to 200 MHz are supported with 5 ns pulse-pair resolution.

Built-in discriminators with adjustable threshold (−300 mV to +300 mV) convert detector pulses to digital counts. Gate scanning sweeps the gate position across the signal, building a time-resolved histogram. GPIB and RS-232 interfaces provide remote control.
