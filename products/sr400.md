The SR400 Dual-Channel Gated Photon Counter offers a convenient, integrated approach to photon counting that avoids the complexity and expense of older counting systems.

### Counters

Two independent channels count at rates up to 200 MHz with gate generators providing counting gates from 5 ns to 1 s. Gate scanning allows the gate position to be swept across the signal, building a time-resolved histogram of photon arrivals.

### Signal Inputs and Discriminators

Analog signal inputs are internally terminated at 50 Ω, accepting signals between ±300 mV with DC to 300 MHz bandwidth. Discriminator thresholds range from −300 mV to +300 mV in 0.2 mV steps with 5 ns pulse-pair resolution.

### Count Periods

Programming supports 1 to 2000 count periods per scan with dwell times from 2 ms to 60 s.

### Outputs and Interfaces

Front panel displays up to 10⁹ counts. D/A output options include A, B, A−B, or A+B in logarithmic or linear scaling. RS-232 and GPIB interfaces with 2000-point internal data buffering are included.
