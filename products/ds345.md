The DS345 is a full-featured 30 MHz synthesized function generator that uses an innovative Direct Digital Synthesis (DDS) architecture.

### Waveform Generation

The DS345 generates sine and square waves up to 30.2 MHz, with triangle and ramp waveforms to 100 kHz. A wideband (10 MHz) white noise source is also provided. All standard waveforms offer 1 µHz frequency resolution.

### Output Specifications

Both function and TTL SYNC outputs feature 50 Ω impedance through floating BNC connectors that may be floated up to ±40 V relative to earth ground. Amplitude ranges from 10 mVpp to 10 Vpp with selectable display units (Vp, Vpp, Vrms, dBm).

### Modulation

The instrument offers amplitude modulation (AM), frequency modulation (FM), and phase modulation (PM) with modulation rates from 1 mHz to 10 kHz. External amplitude modulation is available via rear-panel input.

### Arbitrary Waveforms

The DS345 functions as a full-featured arbitrary waveform generator with 12-bit vertical resolution and playback up to 40 Msamples/s, supporting 8 to 16,300 points.

### Sweeps and Burst

Phase-continuous sweeps are supported across the entire frequency range using linear or logarithmic modes. Tone bursts of any output function except noise can be generated, outputting exact complete waveform cycles following a trigger signal.
