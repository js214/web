The FS752 GNSS Disciplined Time and Frequency Reference provides calibrated time and frequency distribution to your laboratory. A built-in receiver tracks any of the four major GNSS constellations: GPS, GLONASS, BEIDOU, or GALILEO. The instrument comes with a double-oven OCXO timebase that has phase noise of less than -125 dBc/Hz at 10 Hz offset.
