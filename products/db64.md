The SRS Model DB64 Coax Delay Box provides switch-controlled delays from 0 to 63.5 ns with 0.5 ns resolution. Applications include the timing of photomultiplier coincidences, set-up of time-to-amplitude converters, adjustment of signal phase, or any application which requires an adjustable, high-bandwidth analog delay.

The internal construction employs accurate lengths of RG58A/U coax cable with 50 Ω characteristic impedance. DPDT switches allow insertion or removal of delay segments. Careful part selection and circuit board design minimize reflections and switch feed-through for high speed signals. Two units mount side-by-side in a standard 19" rack using the RM1U accessory.
