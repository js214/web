The SG390 Series Vector Signal Generators are high performance, affordable RF sources with carrier frequencies spanning DC to 2.025 GHz (SG392), 4.050 GHz (SG394), and 6.075 GHz (SG396). These instruments incorporate both analog and vector modulation capabilities as standard features.

### Frequency Synthesis

The generators employ Rational Approximation Frequency Synthesis (RAFS) technology, delivering spur-free outputs with low phase noise (−116 dBc/Hz at 1 GHz) and extraordinary frequency resolution (1 µHz at any frequency).

### Analog Modulation

Supports amplitude, frequency, and phase modulation with internal and external sources. The internal modulation generator produces sine, ramp, sawtooth, square, and noise waveforms.

### Vector Modulation

Dual baseband arbitrary waveform generators operating at 125 MHz enable comprehensive digital modulation support including ASK, QPSK, DQPSK, 8PSK, FSK, CPM, QAM (4–256), and VSB variants. Built-in support for GSM, GSM EDGE, W-CDMA, APCO-25, DECT, NADC, PDC, TETRA, and ATSC DTV with automatic configuration.

### Computer Interfaces

GPIB, RS-232, and Ethernet interfaces enable full remote operation and control.
