The SR865A sets a new standard for dual-phase lock-in detection. A 5-inch capacitive touchscreen replaces the knob-driven interfaces of older lock-ins. 4 MHz reference bandwidth, 80 MHz ADC sampling, and a noise floor of 2.5 nV/√Hz make it the instrument of choice for demanding phase-sensitive measurements.

All instrument settings and live data are accessible from the front panel or remotely over Ethernet, USB, or RS-232. An optional GPIB interface is available.
