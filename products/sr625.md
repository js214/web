The SR625 is a 200 MHz frequency counter with a built-in rubidium timebase that delivers extraordinary accuracy without an external reference.

The integrated rubidium oscillator provides 5 × 10⁻¹¹ frequency accuracy and ±2 × 10⁻¹¹ stability (after warm-up), eliminating the need for a separate frequency standard. Aging is less than 5 × 10⁻¹¹ per month.

The counter measures frequency from 0.001 Hz to 200 MHz with up to 11-digit resolution (1 s gate time) and 100 ps period resolution. A 10 MHz timebase output allows other instruments to be referenced to the rubidium standard.

GPIB and RS-232 interfaces provide full remote control.
