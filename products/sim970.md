The SIM970 Quad Digital Voltmeter provides four channels of DC voltage measurement for the SIM900 mainframe.

Each channel provides 7½-digit resolution with input ranges from 100 mV to 10 V full scale. Input impedance is greater than 10 GΩ. The four channels share a single ADC through a multiplexer, with measurement rates up to 14 readings per second per channel.
