The DG645 Digital Delay Generator provides four precision delay channels with less than 25 ps rms jitter. Trigger rates up to 10 MHz and delay resolution of 5 ps make it suitable for the most demanding timing applications.

### Delay Specifications

Delays range from 0 to 2000 seconds with 5 ps resolution. Accuracy is 1 ns plus timebase error times delay. A built-in precision rate generator operates from 100 µHz to 10 MHz at 1 µHz resolution.

### Output Characteristics

Front-panel outputs (T0, AB, CD, EF, GH) provide 50 Ω source impedance with programmable amplitude (0.5–5.0 V) and offset (±2 V) to accommodate various logic levels. Rise times are less than 2 ns.

### Optional Outputs

Option 1 adds 8 rear-panel delay outputs at 5 V CMOS logic. Option 2 provides high-voltage outputs (0–30 V) for noisy environments. Option 3 adds 8 combinatorial outputs with logic OR functions.

### Timebases

Standard crystal, optional OCXO, and optional rubidium timebases are available. Ethernet, GPIB, and RS-232 interfaces provide full remote control.
